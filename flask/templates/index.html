Welcome to the Mental Health Sentiment Analysis App

This project is designed to analyze your mental health statements and predict the sentiment behind them using machine learning techniques.

Benefits of Mental Health Sentiment Analysis:

1. This project can help individuals better understand their mental health by providing insights into their emotional state.

2. Automated sentiment analysis can assist mental health professionals in quickly assessing the mental state of their patients, leading to more efficient and effective treatment plans.
